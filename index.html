A completely independent blockchain and coin technology

CodersCoin is a unique and completely proprietary blockchain and crypto technology. CodersCoin serves a kind of reputational, monetization and thank you purpose for programmers, designers, digital creators and IT enthusiasts. The goal of the development was to provide a simple platform for digital creators to pay each other without the intervention of any banks or governments. Also, digital files can be sent securely. No separate application is required, just a secret phrase and a browser. With CodersCoin, You can empower Your community with fast & secure transactions or support your favorite open source projects.

Cloud wallet

CodersCoin offers a solution where you don't have to worry about technology anymore. Simply create a wallet online with 1 click, and you can start your web3 journey with your unique CodersCoin crypto wallet address!

Safe technology

Each block is interconnected and protected by very strong cryptographic encoding. All transactions that take place within CodersCoin are saved in a large database and distributed among nodes operated by the community. There is no central authority, so your CodersCoins are yours as long as you keep the secret phrase secret.

Lightweight and fast

Although CodersCoin uses a rather complex technology, it does not require huge computing capacity like all other cryptocurrencies. You can easily set up a new node or even start mining CSC even on a Raspberry Pi!

Made for IT enthusiasts

CodersCoin was made by developers for developers, designers and digital creators. You can easily paste your CSC balance into your website or any README.md file. Your entire CodersCoin wallet can be integrated with Github.

Access the wallet

With CodersCoin, there is no need for an external wallet application, as the wallet itself is stored online and can be assigned to your Github account. Every time you log into the online wallet with your Github account or secret phrase, your CSC balance will be displayed. It is also possible to transfer and receive CodersCoin within the wallet.
