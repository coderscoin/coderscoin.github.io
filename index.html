A completely independent blockchain and coin technology

CodersCoin is a unique and completely proprietary blockchain and crypto technology. CodersCoin serves a kind of reputational and thank you purpose for programmers, designers and IT enthusiasts. The goal of the development was to provide a simple platform for digital creators to pay each other without the intervention of any banks or governments. Also, digital files can be sent securely. No separate application is required, just a Github account. With CodersCoin, You can empower Your community with fast & secure transactions or support your favorite open source projects.

Cloud wallet

CodersCoin offers a solution where you no longer need to register to create a wallet. All your transactions are assigned to your Github account.

Safe technology

Each block is interconnected and protected by very strong cryptographic encoding. All transactions that take place within CodersCoin are saved in a large database and distributed among nodes operated by the community.

Lightweight and fast

Although CodersCoin uses a rather complex technology, it does not require huge computing capacity like all other cryptocurrencies.

Made for IT enthusiasts

CodersCoin was made by developers for developers. You can easily paste your CSC balance into any README.md or HTML document. The entire blockchain and cryptocurrency works within Github.

Access the wallet

With CodersCoin, there is no need for an external wallet application, as the wallet itself will be assigned to your Github account. Every time you log into the online wallet with your Github account, your CSC balance will be displayed. It is also possible to transfer and receive CodersCoin within the wallet.
